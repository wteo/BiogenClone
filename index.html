What is Red Light Therapy?

Red light therapy, also known as low-level laser therapy (LLLT) or photobiomodulation, is a treatment that uses low wavelength red light to improve your skin's appearance, such as reducing wrinkles, scars, redness, and acne. It's also used to treat other medical conditions.

Our treatments help with:

  • Enhancing cellular energy production
  • Reducing inflammation
  • Improving muscle recovery
  • Supporting skin health and appearance
  • Promoting overall wellness
