Terms & Conditions

Appointment Packs

Appointment packs are non-refundable and non-transferable. Appointment packs are only valid for use by the client who purchased them and cannot be shared. Appointment packs expire after a period of 24 months from date of first use.

Cancellation Policy

Appointments with our laser treatment must be cancelled or moved 6 hours prior to the start of the scheduled appointment. Appointments cannot be moved after this time and one session will be removed from the client's pack regardless of whether the client uses the pod.

Contraindications To Use

  1. Our equipment should not be used while pregnant. There is no evidence to suggest that red light therapy can harm the unborn foetus but it is a time when we suggest clients should simplify their personal health strategy and focus on rest and good nutrition alone.
  2. Age: We suggest our equipment should not be used by minors under the age of 18 without a direct referral from a medical practitioner for a specific dysfunction or disease.
